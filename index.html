Instructions for Gameplay

This is an emulation of the classic game TRON. You are a driver of a light-bike on a grid facing off against an opponent. Your bike creates a wall, blocking off possible routes for both your opponent and yourself. Your objective is to outlast your opponent on the board, navigating the gameboard as the playable area gets smaller. Each game is a best 2 out of 3 rounds. Good Luck

Controls

  • Player 1
  • Up: W
  • Down: S
  • Left: A
  • Right: D
  • Player 2 Uses Arrow Keys
